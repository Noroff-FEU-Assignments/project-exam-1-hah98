LOVE & UNION ORGIN STORY

Love & Union is an online blog, magazine and community. Originally Love & Union was created
as a personal blog in late 1998 by our founder H. Haha in Oslo, Norway. By the year 2000, after gaining significant traffic,
blog turned into a magazine. In the year 2015 the L&U team decided to move from online and physical magazine back to only online.
